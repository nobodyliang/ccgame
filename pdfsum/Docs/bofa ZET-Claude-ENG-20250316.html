Key Investment Thesis

Server Business Expansion

Server sales nearly doubled YoY to CNY10bn in 2024 (8% mix), led by breakthrough at China CSPs and rising delivery to telcos. ZTE also started shipment of AI server from 4Q24.

In-house CPU Advantage

ZTE is one of the few vendors in China that can provide both in-house CPU and system design. This strengthens ZTE's competitiveness amid domestic replacement demand.

Valuation Re-rating

Despite 10-20% correction from Feb, ZTE trades below the last peak in 1H23. Southbound shows strong holding power for H-share at 53% and short interest down to 10%.

Conclusion & Investment Recommendation

Key Takeaways

  • Upgrade to Buy with new POs of HK$32/CNY45, lifting valuation from previous HK$21/CNY31
  • We see server business as a key growth driver, with near doubling of sales to CNY10bn in 2024
  • In-house CPU capability provides competitive advantage in China's domestic replacement trend
  • Despite carrier business weakness (well known by market), we see valuation re-rating supported by China IDC's positive outlook
  • Strong Southbound holding power (53%) and diminishing short interest (10% vs. 30% peak) provide support
