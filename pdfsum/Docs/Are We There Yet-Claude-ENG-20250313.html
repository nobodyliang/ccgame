Market Analysis March 2025

Market Overview

Since its high on February 18, the MSCI World index has fallen 4.6% while the S&P 500 is down 6.2% and just below its 200-day average which it has been above since November 2023. The Nasdaq has dropped 9.2% with bellwether Nvidia down 20.7%.

Key insight: Although the S&P 500 is down 5.1% since the CGTSPOLL indicator triggered on February 12, the average decline is 5.9% with a standard deviation of 4.2%. This means there's around a 15% probability of a 10.1% decline.

Cryptocurrency Outlook

Ahead of the White House conference, Bitcoin's decline is still below expected levels when the CGTSPOLL hits 18. The average decline in Bitcoin is 21.0%, but so far its maximum fall is only 13.8% since February 12.

Key observation: There is still hope in this market and risk barometers are not yet at extreme levels. Many regional and global equities have very high correlations with Bitcoin and NVIDIA.

Conclusion & Recommendations

For the time being it is too early to buy. Continue selling into bounces.

Recommended Hedges

Focus hedges on markets like the Eurostoxx, HSI, and FTSE which have not yet corrected and where long positions are likely to be more vulnerable.

What to Watch For

The market low is likely to be marked by a rise in intermarket and interasset correlation. US10yr yields may drop down to around 3.95% by the time this sell-off is finished.
