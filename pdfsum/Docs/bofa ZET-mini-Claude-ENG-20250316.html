Investment Overview

Upgrade to Buy from Neutral on server take-off

We upgrade ZTE-H/ZTE-A to Buy from Neutral, with new price objectives of HK$32/CNY45. The server business saw robust expansion in 2024, as China telco/CSP orders took off. Though earnings may be under pressure near term due to soft carrier business (~50% mix), we believe ZTE deserves a re-rating.

Key Investment Catalysts:

  • China IDCs' positive outlook
  • Share-gain potential amid increasing server demand
  • In-house CPU capability providing competitive edge

After 10-20% correction from February, ZTE-H/ZTE-A still trades below the last peak in 1H23 (led by AI sentiment) and at a discount to China server peers. We see the valuation supported by China IDC's re-rating given market's rising confidence on server demand.

Key Investment Findings

Server Business Growth

ZTE's server sales nearly doubled YoY to CNY10bn in 2024 (8% mix), led by breakthrough at China CSPs and rising delivery to telcos. The company started AI server shipments from 4Q24.

Growth Drivers:

  • CSPs' strong capex (BAT +64%/19% in 2025/26E)
  • China telcos' increasing focus on IDC
  • Rising demand for AI inferencing capabilities
Server Sales Growth (YoY) +95%

In-house CPU Advantage

ZTE is one of the few vendors in China that can provide both in-house CPU and system design. This strengthens ZTE's competitiveness amid domestic replacement demand.

Key Advantages:

  • Well-regulated under US's 10-year compliance supervision (till 2028)
  • Edge in accessing advanced semiconductors vs. other China peers
  • Zhufeng CPU (7nm) provides total solution capability
In-house products utilization 85-90%

Market Analysis

Valuation Analysis

We lift ZTE-H's PO to HK$32 (13x 2026E P/E) from HK$21 (8x 2025E P/E), and lift ZTE-A's PO to CNY45 (20x 2026E P/E) from CNY31 (13x 2025E P/E).

We roll over ZTE-H's/ZTE-A's valuation base to 2026 and apply a higher multiple of 13x/20x, matching the trading range seen during the last AI-driven market in 1H23.

Valuation Drivers:

  • Server delivery momentum
  • Market's positive outlook on China IDC industry
  • Southbound holding percentage risen to 53%
  • Short interest dropped to 10% (from high of 20-30%)
