Key Market Insights

US Faces Worst Earnings Revision Breadth

Monthly data analysis shows US markets experiencing deteriorating earnings revision breadth compared to other major markets.

Source: IBES, Datastream, Morgan Stanley Research. Data as of February 2025.

Valuations Beginning to Converge

Historical P/E ratios show a narrowing gap between US and emerging markets, suggesting a potential shift in market dynamics.

Source: IBES, MSCI, FactSet, Morgan Stanley Research. Data as of March 10, 2025.

US as ROE Outlier - Potential Vulnerability

The US market shows exceptional ROE compared to other global markets, raising questions about sustainability if ROE falls.

Source: FactSet, MSCI, RIMES, Morgan Stanley Research. Data as of March 10, 2025.

Regional Exposure & Sector Analysis

Asia/EM Revenue Exposure to North America

Taiwan and South Korea show highest revenue dependency on North American markets, creating potential vulnerability.

Source: Company data, Morgan Stanley Research.

Asia-Pacific Semiconductors - Elevated Valuations

P/Sales ratios remain high compared to historical averages, with clear inverse relationship to subsequent returns.

Source: MSCI, FactSet, Morgan Stanley Research. Data as of March 10, 2025.

Sector Recommendations

Financials vs. Information Technology

Morgan Stanley currently prefers Financials over IT within the EM space, based on valuation and growth prospects.

Source: FactSet, Morgan Stanley Research. Data as of March 10, 2025.

Key Strategic Takeaway

As US market revisions deteriorate and valuations converge, Morgan Stanley sees improved risk-reward in select EM markets, with preference for Financials over IT sectors. Taiwan and South Korea warrant caution due to high North American revenue exposure.
